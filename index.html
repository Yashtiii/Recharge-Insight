About RechargeInsight

RechargeInsight is a geospatial decision support platform developed to identify groundwater recharge suitability zones across Kanpur Nagar and Kanpur Dehat using multi-criteria environmental analysis.

Project Objective

The system helps planners and researchers identify suitable locations for groundwater recharge structures such as check dams, percolation tanks and recharge pits.

Why This Matters

Kanpur faces groundwater depletion and localized contamination challenges. Existing recharge studies often ignore contamination risk. RechargeInsight attempts to incorporate this factor into suitability analysis.

Factors Considered

Suitability is evaluated using eight environmental factors:

  • Soil Texture
  • Slope
  • Rainfall
  • Groundwater Level
  • Land Use/Land Cover
  • Geology
  • Drainage Density
  • Contamination Risk

Contamination Risk Modeling

Contamination risk was approximated using built-up land cover derived from Dynamic World V1 (2023). Built-up areas were considered higher risk zones due to potential industrial and urban pollution sources.

This serves as a proxy indicator due to the lack of high-resolution industrial contamination datasets.

Method Overview

All factors were standardized and combined using multi-criteria suitability analysis to produce five recharge suitability zones.

Suitability Classes

  • Very Good – Highly suitable recharge zones
  • Good – Suitable with minor constraints
  • Moderate – Conditional recharge potential
  • Poor – Limited suitability
  • Very Poor – Not recommended

Limitations

  • Built-up layer used as contamination proxy
